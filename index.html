Encrypted local bookmark vault for private links, research references, documentation, admin panels, onion addresses, read-later queues, and browser bookmark import/export.

Encrypted local address book for private contacts, relationship fields, badges, birthdays, important dates, and vCard import/export.

[ OPEN_LEDGER ]

Bunker Planner
